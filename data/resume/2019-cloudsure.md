Cloudsure offers a white-labeled digital insurance platform to financial service providers. One of their biggest partners is Simply Financial Services. I started as a Senior Software Engineer at Simply Financial Services, moved to the partner company (Cloudsure Limited) based in Mauritius and was promoted to team lead on 1 November 2022.

## Leadership & Team Management

- Co-led a team of 15 engineers across two time zones, fostering a collaborative and high-performing environment.
- Onboarded, mentored and guided engineers, improving their skills and the overall quality of code delivered.
- Demonstrated strong leadership qualities through empathy and active listening, resulting in improved team communication, collaboration, and morale.
- Managed the support team, shaving turnaround times down to a maximum of 3 days thus improving user satisfaction.

## Software Development & Architecture

- Navigated, developed, and architected complex distributed systems on GCP, encompassing over 10 repositories and 8 running applications.
- Cross pollinated learnings through code reviews, documentation and communication.
- Reviewed Merge Requests daily, ensuring code quality and adherence to programming guidelines.
- Maintained a keen eye for detail in UX and DevX, contributing to improved user and developer experiences.

## Business Impact & Problem Solving

- Played a key role in onboarding new partners. Example, contributed to a 60% increase in company revenue in a 4 month project.
- Contributed to the bigger picture through systems thinking by architecting and designing features that truly matter.
- Proactively identified and resolved critical issues prior to product launches, mitigating potential risks.
- Effectively managed high-pressure situations and resolve critical incidents, ensuring system stability.
- Fixed issues at source, writing up and sharing post-mortems to prevent future challenges.
