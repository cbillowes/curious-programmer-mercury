  When working with Clojure data structures, you may need to redact certain values.
  Here's a quick snippet on how to do just that using clojure.walk/postwalk.
---

```bash
(defn redact
  [data]
  (walk/postwalk
   (fn [node]
     (if (and (vector? node) (= :id-number (first node)))
       [:id-number "REDACTED"]
       node))
   data))
```
