When it comes to image editing, many people immediately think of Adobe Photoshop.
However, Photoshop can be rather expensive.

If you're looking for a free alternative that doesn't require any installation, nor an account, [Photopea](https://www.photopea.com/) is an excellent choice.

Photopea is a web-based image editor that offers a wide range of features similar to Photoshop.
It supports various file formats, including PSD, XCF, Sketch, and more.

You can use Photopea for tasks such as photo retouching, graphic design, and even creating web graphics.

The interface is user-friendly and closely resembles that of Photoshop, making it easy for those familiar with Photoshop to get started.

One of the standout features of Photopea is its ability to work directly in your browser.
This means you don't need to download or install any software, and you can access your projects from any device with an internet connection.

Additionally, Photopea is free to use, although there is a premium version available that removes ads and offers additional features.

Overall, Photopea is a powerful and convenient tool for anyone in need of a free image editing solution.
Whether you're a professional designer or just someone looking to make quick edits to your photos, Photopea is definitely worth checking out.
